About us!!


India has a long tradition in farming. For centuries farmers have been the backbone of our economy. we believe enabling and empowering our farmers and their immediate family will have a significant positive impact to our economy and society as whole. Appropriate information sharing (Educate), and engaging the farmers empowers them.

E-Kisaan Application is a not-for-profit organization. We focus on leveraging technology to improve agriculture.

We believe in simpler solutions to address common challenges. Existing ideas combined with new techniques provide effective solutions.We believe everyone has ideas, that can make a difference. If you would like to contribute to this project, mail us your ideas with your contact details, we look forward to hearing from you.

Government Schemes

Govt. schemes are the schemes to safeguard farmers from financial loss occurring due to non-preventable natural risks.

MSP - Minimum Support Price

MSP is price fixed by Government of India to protect the producer - farmers - against excessive fall in price during bumper production years.
